About Universitas Teknologi Bandung

Universitas Teknologi Bandung (UTB) was founded in 1991, previously named Sekolah Tinggi Teknologi Bandung (STTB) until March 2024. UTB has a vision and mission to become a pillar of innovation and excellence in technology education. UTB has 5 majors, including Industrial Engineering, Informatics Engineering, Visual Communication Design, Digital Business, and most recently Retail Management.

Why Choose UTB?

UTB has 5 college majors which are popular majors in the industrial revolution 5.0

Industrial Engineering

Industrial Engineering is a field that studies how to design, manage, and improve the performance of integrated systems. The system consists of humans, machines, materials, environment, energy, information, and financial analysis.

Informatics Engineering

Informatics engineering is a scientific field that studies how to use computer technology to solve problems related to data processing and transformation. It involves the study of computer technology, including software development, testing, evaluation, operating systems, and mathematical analysis.

Visual Communication Design

Visual communication design is the process of using visual elements to convey information and ideas. It's a field that combines art and storytelling to create a message that can inspire action or evoke an emotional response. Visual communication designers use a variety of visual mediums, including typography, drawing, illustration, graphic design, and animation, to create artwork that informs, enlightens, and inspires their target audience.

Digital Business

A digital business is a business that utilizes digital technology in various aspects of its operations, such as production, distribution, marketing and customer service. Digital technology is not only used to support existing processes, but also to create value, innovate and achieve greater efficiency.

NEW!!! Retail Management

Retail management is the process of organizing, planning, controlling and utilizing resources in a retail business. The goal is for retail trade to run well and meet customer needs.
